About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! I am currently a UBC student majoring in the the fields of Statistics and Finance. After spending 3 years studying Statistics and consulting in Japan, I became captivated by the field of quantitative analysis. As my statistical skills became more developed, I began applying them to the areas of Finance and Economics. Integrating my knowledge from both disciplines has opened many doors and enabled me to obtain a fascinating research position at UBC. My current areas of interest involve big data and using stochastic processes to model macroeconomic crises. With my business background, I understood that communicating my findings to others was just as important as analysing them.
