About Me

Hello and welcome to my page!
I am a student at the University of British Columbia, located in the City of Vancouver, Canada. I specialise in the area of Finance and Statistics, with the aim of intertwining financial theory with statistical modelling. My current research interests include dynamic computer experiments and stochastic modelling of financial and economic data. At the moment, I am using stochastic computer experiments on a large economic data set to simulate various global and environmental scenerios.

When I'm not behind the computer, I find myself travelling across the globe looking for an adventure. I am an avid traveller, which compliments my fascination with learning languages. Communication in this day and age is vital - if your going to explain it in a complicated manner, I'd rather you not explain it at all.
